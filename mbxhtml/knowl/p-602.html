Consider the coordinate transformation \begin{equation*} \vec T(r,\theta) = (r\cos\theta,r\sin\theta). \end{equation*}

  1. Let \(r=3\) and then graph \(\vec T(3,\theta)=(3\cos\theta,3\sin\theta)\) for \(\theta\in[0,2\pi]\text{.}\)

  2. Let \(\theta=\frac{\pi}{4}\) and then, on the same axes as above, add the graph of \(\vec T\left(r,\frac{\pi}{4}\right)=\left(r\frac{\sqrt 2}{2},r \frac{\sqrt 2}{2}\right)\) for \(r\in[0,5]\text{.}\)
