EXTRA 6 Please use this Sage link to check your work. Consider the parabolic curve \(y=4-x^2\) for \(-1\leq x\leq 2\text{,}\) and the vector field \(\vec F(x,y) = (2x+y,-x)\text{.}\)

  1. Give a parametrization \(\vec r(t)\) of the parabolic curve that starts at \((-1,3)\) and ends at \((2,0)\text{.}\) See the review exercise above if you need a hint.

  2. Compute \(d\vec r\) and state \(dx\) and \(dy\text{.}\)

  3. What are \(M\) and \(N\) in terms of \(t\text{?}\) Hint

    Use \(\vec r (t)\) and substitution

  4. Compute the work done by \(\vec F\) to move an object along the parabola from \((-1,3)\) to \((2,0)\) (i.e. compute \(\int _C Mdx+Ndy\)). Check your answer with Sage.

  5. How much work is done by \(\vec F\) to move an object along the parabola from \((2,0)\) to \((-1,3)\text{.}\)

  6. In general, if you traverse along a path backwards, how much work is done?
