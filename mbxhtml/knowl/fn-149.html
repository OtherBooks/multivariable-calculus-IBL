The velocity is \(\vec v(t) = (3\sin t, -4\cos t)\text{.}\). The speed is \(v(t) = \sqrt{9\sin^2t+16\cos^2t}\text{.}\). The distance traveled is the arc length \(\ds s=\int_0^{2\pi} \left(\sqrt{9\sin^2t+16\cos^2t}\right)dt\text{.}\). Since the horse's initial and final position are equal, the displacement is zero. Arc length does not equal displacement. in-context
