Exercise6.1.7

In the plane,

  1. Graph the curve \(y=\sin x\) for \(x\in[0,2\pi]\) (make an \(x,y\) table)

  2. Graph the curve \(r=\sin\theta\) for \(\theta\in[0,2\pi]\) (an \(r,\theta\) table).

The graphs should look very different. If one looks like a circle, you're on the right track.
