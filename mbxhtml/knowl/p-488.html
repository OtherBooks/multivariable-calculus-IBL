  1. What are \(n\) and \(m\) when we write this function in the form \(\vec r\colon {\mathbb{R}}^n\to {\mathbb{R}}^m\text{?}\)

  2. Construct a graph of this function.

  3. Next to a few points on your graph, include the time \(t\) at which the horse is at this point on the graph. Include an arrow for the horse's direction.
