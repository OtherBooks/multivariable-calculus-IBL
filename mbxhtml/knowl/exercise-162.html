Exercise9.3.4

Consider the curve \(\vec r(t)=(a\cos t, a\sin t)\text{.}\)

  1. Draw the curve, and state the radius \(\rho\) of the best approximating circle.

  2. Find the curvature vector \(\vec \kappa\) by performing a computation.

  3. What relationship exists between \(\rho\) and \(\kappa\text{?}\) If the radius \(\rho\) were to increase, what would happen to \(\kappa\text{?}\)
