EXTRA 5 \thomasee{See 11.3: 53-66.}Each of the following equations is written in the Cartesian (rectangular) coordinate system. Convert each to an equation in polar coordinates by substituting in the formulas from 6.1.5, and then solve for \(r\) so that the equation is in the form \(r=f(\theta)\text{.}\).

  1. \(x^2+y^2=7\)

  2. \(2x+3y=5\)

  3. \(x^2=y\)
