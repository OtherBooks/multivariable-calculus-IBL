When we found average value, we wanted a height \(\bar f\) such that the area under \(f\) and the area under \(\bar f\) were the same. As an equation, we wrote \begin{equation*} \iint_R \bar f dA = \iint_R f dA, \end{equation*} and then since \(\bar f\) is constant, we pulled it out of the integral, and then solved for \(\bar f\) to get \begin{equation*} \bar f \iint_R dA = \iint_R f dA \text{ or } \bar f = \frac{\iint_R f dA }{\iint_R dA} . \end{equation*}
