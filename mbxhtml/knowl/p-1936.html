Verify Stokes' theorem by computing both sides of the equation: \begin{equation*} \int_C\vec F\cdot d\vec r = \iint_S \text{ curl } \cdot \vec n d\sigma. \end{equation*} The left side will require you to set up and evaluate a line integral. The right side will require you to evaluate a surface integral (though you should be able to reduce this integral to a known quantity).
