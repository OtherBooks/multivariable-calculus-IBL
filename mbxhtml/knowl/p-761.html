If you know that a line passes through the point \((1,2,3)\) and is parallel to the vector \((4,5,6)\text{,}\), give a vector equation, and parametric equations, of the line. See  6 A vector equation is \(\vec r(t) = (4,5,6)t+(1,2,3)\) or \(\vec r(t) = (4t+1, 5t+2, 6t+3)\text{.}\) Parametric equations for this line are \(x=4t+1\text{,}\) \(y=5t+2\text{,}\) and \(z=6t+3\text{.}\) for an answer.
