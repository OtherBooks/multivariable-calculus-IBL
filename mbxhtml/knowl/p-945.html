EXTRA 5 Watch a YouTube video. Answer the following questions. Assume we fire a projectile from the origin, which means the acceleration, velocity, and position are \begin{equation*} \vec a(t) = (0,-g), \vec v(t) = (v_{x_0}, -gt+ v_{y_0}), \vec r(t) = (v_{x_0}t, -\frac12 gt^2+ v_{y_0}t) . \end{equation*}

  1. What's the time to max height? What's the flight time?

  2. Show why the maximum height is \(\ds y_{\max}=\frac{v_{y_0}^2}{2g}\) and the range is \(\ds R=\frac{2v_{x_0}v_{y_0}}{g}\text{.}\)

  3. If the initial speed is \(v_0\text{,}\) with a firing angle of \(\alpha\) above the horizontal, rewrite \(v_{x_0}\) and \(v_{y_0}\) in terms of \(v_0\) and \(\alpha\text{,}\) and then state the range in terms of \(v_0\) and \(\alpha\text{.}\)
