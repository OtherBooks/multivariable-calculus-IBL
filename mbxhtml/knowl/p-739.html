Consider the parametric surface \(\vec r(u,v) = (u,v,v\cos(uv))\text{.}\). Compute both \(\ds\frac{\partial \vec r}{\partial u}\) and \(\ds\frac{\partial \vec r}{\partial v}\text{.}\). Thenm state \(D\vec r(u,v)\text{.}\). If you end up with a 3 by 2 matrix, you did this correctly.
