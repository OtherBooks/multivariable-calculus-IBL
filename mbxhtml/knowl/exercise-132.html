Exercise8.2.8

Compute the requested partial and total derivatives.

  1. Consider the parametric surface \(\vec r(u,v) = (u,v,v\cos(uv))\text{.}\) Compute both \(\ds\frac{\partial \vec r}{\partial u}\) and \(\ds\frac{\partial \vec r}{\partial v}\text{.}\) Thenm state \(D\vec r(u,v)\text{.}\) If you end up with a 3 by 2 matrix, you did this correctly.

  2. Consider the vector field \(\vec F(x,y) = (-y,xe^{3y})\text{.}\) Compute both \(\ds\frac{\partial \vec F}{\partial x}\) and \(\ds\frac{\partial \vec F}{\partial y}\text{.}\) Then state \(D\vec F(x,y)\text{.}\)
