Exercise14.2.5

Consider the region \(D\) in space that is inside both the sphere \(x^2+y^2+z^2=9\) and the cylinder \(x^2+y^2=4\text{.}\)

Hint

Start by drawing the region.

  1. Set up an iterated integral in Cartesian (rectangular) coordinates that would give the volume of \(D\text{.}\)

  2. Set up an iterated integral in cylindrical coordinates that would give the volume of \(D\text{.}\)
