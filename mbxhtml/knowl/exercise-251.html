Exercise12.5.3

EXTRA 2 \bmw{See 16.4 for more practice. Try doing a bunch of these, as they get really fast.} Consider a rectangle \(C\) where the boundary of the rectangle are \(2\leq x\leq 7\) and \(0\leq y\leq 3\text{.}\) The rectangle is subject to a vector field \(\vec F=(2x+3y,4x+5y)\text{.}\)

  1. Compute \(N_x-M_y\)

  2. Compute \(M_x+N_y\text{.}\)

  3. Find the circulation of \(\vec F\) along \(C\)

  4. Find the flux of \(\vec F\) along \(C\)

Hint

The last two should reduce to facts about area.

Note: If you tried doing this without Green's theorem, you would have to parametrize 4 line segments, compute 4 integrals, and then sum the results.
