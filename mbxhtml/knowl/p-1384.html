EXTRA 5 Wikipedia has some interesting applications of center of mass.The center of mass of an object is the point where the object balances. In order to calculate the \(x\)-coordinate of the center of mass, we average the \(x\)-coordinates, but we weight each \(x\)-coordinate with its mass. Similarly, we can calculate the \(y\) and \(z\) coordinates of the center of mass.
