  • The divergence, or flux density, of \(\vec F\) at \((x,y)\) we define to be \begin{equation*} \frac{\partial M}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial N}{\partial y}=M_x+N_y=\lim_{a\to 0} \frac{1}{A_a}\oint_{C_a} \vec F \cdot \vec n ds = \lim_{a\to 0} \frac{1}{A_a}\oint_{C_a} Mdy-Ndx. \end{equation*}
