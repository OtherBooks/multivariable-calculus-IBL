For each region \(R\) below, draw the region and give a set of inequalities of the form \(a\leq x\leq b, c(x)\leq y\leq d(x)\) or in the form \(c\lt y\lt d, a(y)\leq x\leq b(y)\text{.}\). In class, we'll give whichever one you did not.

  1. The region \(R\) is above the line \(x+y=1\) and inside the circle \(x^2+y^2=1\text{.}\)

  2. The region \(R\) is below the line \(y=8\text{,}\) above the curve \(y=x^2\text{,}\) and to the right of the \(y\)-axis.

  3. The region \(R\) is bounded by \(2x+y=3\text{,}\) \(y=x\text{,}\) and \(x=0\text{.}\)
