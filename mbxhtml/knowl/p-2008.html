The spherical change of coordinates is: \begin{align*} x\amp =\rho\sin\phi\cos\theta,\ y=\rho\sin\phi\sin\theta,\ z=\rho\cos\phi\\ \text{ or in vector form } \amp \\\ \vec S(\rho,\phi,\theta) \amp = (\rho\sin\phi\cos\theta,\rho\sin\phi\sin\theta,\rho\cos\phi). \end{align*}
