If you are standing at \((2,1,-3)\) and you wish to move 6 units in the direction of the unit vector \((1/3, 2/3, -2/3)\text{,}\), where are you? See  5 We want to start at \((2,1,-3)\) and move \(6(1/3, 2/3, -2/3) = (2,4,-4)\text{.}\) We just add the vectors, giving \((4,5,-7)\text{.}\) for an answer.
