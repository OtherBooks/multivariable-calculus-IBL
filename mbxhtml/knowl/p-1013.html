  1. Replace \(t\) with \(5t\) and compute \(\vec v\text{,}\) \(\vec a\text{,}\) \(\vec T\text{,}\) \(\dfrac{d\vec T}{dt}\text{,}\) \(\dfrac{d\vec T}{ds}\text{,}\) and \(\left|\dfrac{d\vec T}{ds}\right|\text{.}\)

  2. If we still want to evaluate at the time corresponding to the point \((0,3)\text{,}\) what is \(t=\text{?.}\) \begin{hint}It should be smaller than \(\frac{\pi}{2}\)

  4. Explain why we should evaluate at \(t=\frac{\pi}{2\omega}\) for the point \((0,3)\text{.}\)
