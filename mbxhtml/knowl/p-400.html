Explain why the length of the path given by \(\vec r(t)\) for \(a\leq t\leq b\) is EXTRA 5 This is the arc length formula. Ask me in class for an alternate way to derive this formula. \begin{equation*} \text{ Arc Length } =s=\int ds=\int_a^b \left|\frac{d\vec r}{dt}\right| dt=\int_a^b \sqrt{\left(\frac{dx}{dt}\right)^2+\left(\frac{dy}{dt}\right)^2}dt. \end{equation*}
