Hint9.5.2.1

We know that \(\ds \vec a = a_T\vec T+a_N\vec N=\frac{d}{dt}|\vec v| \vec T + \kappa |\vec v|^2 \vec N.\) Cross both sides with \(\vec v\text{.}\) You should be able to cancel \(\vec v\times \vec v\) (why). Then take the magnitude of each side and solve for \(\kappa\text{.}\) You'll have to explain why \(|\vec v\times \vec N| = |\vec v|\text{.}\)
