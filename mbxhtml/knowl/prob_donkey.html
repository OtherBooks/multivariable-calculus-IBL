EXTRA 3 \thomasee{See 11.1: 3,4.} Consider the vector \(\vec v=\langle 3,-1 \rangle\text{.}\)

  1. Draw \(\vec v\text{,}\) \(-\vec v\text{,}\) and \(3\vec v\text{.}\)

Suppose a donkey travels along the path given by \((x,y)=\vec v t = (3t,-t)\text{,}\) where \(t\) represents time.

  1. At the following times, where is the donkey?

    1. \(t=0\) ?

    2. \(t=1\) ?

    3. \(t=2\) ?

  2. Draw an axis, then sketch the path followed by the donkey.

  3. Where is the donkey at time \(t=0,1,2\text{?}\) Put markers with labels on your graph to show the donkey's location.
