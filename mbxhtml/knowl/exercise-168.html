Exercise9.4.6

Answer the following questions (this will review your knowledge of the dot and cross products).

  1. What is \(\vec T\cdot \vec N\text{?}\) Explain.

  2. Now explain why \(\vec T\cdot \vec B=0\) and \(\vec N\cdot \vec B=0\text{.}\)

  3. Both \(\vec T\) and \(\vec N\) are unit vectors. They are also orthogonal. Why is \(\vec B\) is a unit vector? [What's the connection between the cross product and area.]

  4. We defined \(\vec B=\vec T\times \vec N\text{.}\) This means that \(\vec N\times \vec T=-\vec B\text{.}\) Do we have \(\vec T\times \vec B\) equal to \(\vec N\) or \(-\vec N\text{?}\) Explain. Hint

    What does the right hand rule say? Compare to \(\hat i\times \hat k\text{.}\)
